DOCUMENT 02 / CLOSURE ARTEFACT / 11 JULY 2017

Discharge Summary

The document that preserved uncertainty while performing diagnostic and administrative closure.

PUBLIC GUIDE

This is the closure document. It is where uncertainty and finality occupy the same record.

The discharge summary is the most important public document because it does two incompatible things at once. It records reasons to keep the diagnostic sequence open, while also participating in the creation of finality.

A discharge summary is not merely descriptive. It is a handover instrument. It tells future readers what has been decided, what remains active, and what frame should govern care. When such a document contains unresolved uncertainty but is used as a settled endpoint, the contradiction becomes structural.

The discharge summary recorded incompleteness and performed completion.

The document preserved atypical features. It preserved the unresolved status of the feet. It preserved caution about dopaminergic therapy. It preserved the possibility of later regret. It preserved questions around the exact nature of the Parkinsonian syndrome. It also moved the patient out of the inpatient setting with the governing diagnostic frame effectively installed.

This is why the document cannot safely be treated as ordinary medical uncertainty. Ordinary uncertainty should restrain finality. Here, uncertainty remained in the prose while finality governed the pathway.

If the record was open, closure was premature. If closure was proper, the later claim of openness is cosmetic.

The public question is therefore simple. Where, inside this document, is the initiating foot-centred problem resolved, falsified, or explicitly superseded? Where is the point at which the original referral problem loses governing force for a documented reason? Where is the sequence completed before reliance is created?

If those answers are absent, then the discharge summary is not just a clinical narrative. It is an artefact of premature closure.

DOCUMENT FUNCTION

The discharge summary is where open language meets closed consequence.

It preserves warning signs.

It performs finality.
