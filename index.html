PUBLIC RECORD ARCHIVE / CASE FILE 2017-2026

Finality
Before
Inquiry

A documentary case file on premature certainty, diagnostic foreclosure, and the record that could not correct itself.

This is not a story about disagreement with a diagnosis.

It is a record of what happens when a system becomes certain before it has earned the right to be certain.

CORE DISCLOSURE

The truth was already in the files.

The documents were never silent. They recorded the foot pain. They recorded atypicality. They recorded dystonic features. They recorded caution. They recorded uncertainty.

Then the institution acted in finality anyway.

If no one could know, no one could certify as if they knew.

GOVERNING FINDING

The record speaks against itself.

The case does not require the reader to believe the patient over the documents.

It requires the reader to believe the documents over the institution's later use of them.

The institution cannot be provisional when challenged and final when relied upon.
